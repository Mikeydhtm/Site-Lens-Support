Help & Support

We're here to help

Having a problem or got a suggestion? Reach out any time.

How do I delete a project?

On the home screen, tap the trash icon on any project card. You'll be asked to confirm before anything is deleted — so no accidental losses.

How do I add my own survey conditions?

When creating a new project, choose "Start Fresh" to build your own conditions from scratch, or select "Use Standard Conditions" and add extras within each section of the survey.

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays on your device. Site Lens doesn't use any external servers — your projects, notes, and photos never leave your iPhone or iPad.

How do I export and share my survey?

Open a project and tap the export icon. From there you can share all your survey notes and photos in one go using iOS's standard share sheet — email, AirDrop, Messages, and more.

Can I update my name or job title?

Yes — tap the gear icon in the bottom-right corner of the home screen to open Settings, where you can update your profile at any time.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Site Lens handles your information. Our app is designed from the ground up to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. All data entered into the app is stored locally on your device only. Examples of data stored locally include:

  • Project photos

This information never leaves your device.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using your device's internal storage. We do not operate servers and we do not transmit your information to any external service.

Data Access

We cannot access, view, or retrieve any data you enter into the application. It is entirely private to you.

Third Party Services

Site Lens does not use analytics tracking, advertising networks, or third-party data collection tools of any kind.
